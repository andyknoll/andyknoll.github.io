About Rocco Osteria

Originally opened in 1967 as the "Monroe Bistro", the current Rocco restaurant strives to maintain the original "Little Italy" look and feel of original owner Mario Penzale.

For over 50 years, this establishment has provided a unique piece of the Old Country right in the heart of downtown Rochester, NY. Best known for classic Italian pizza and hearty dishes, Rocco Osteria makes sure to throw some "New World" flair into the mix.

Current owner Marc Assini is a grandson of Mario and welcomes the responsibility of carrying on the family tradition - Molto Rocco!
